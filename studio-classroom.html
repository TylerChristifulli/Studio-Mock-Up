Understanding COPD

Treatment & Scenarios · Lesson 2 of 3
schedule0.5 CE hr · Airway, Respiratory & Ventilation (F3) verifiedANCCverifiedCAPCE KentuckyTennesseeVirginia
This course reviews the fundamentals of COPD pathophysiology and the evidence-based treatment of the COPD patient — from recognizing airflow limitation and gas trapping to prioritizing interventions in real transport scenarios.

By the end of this lesson you will be able to:

  1. Define the core pathophysiologic mechanisms of COPD, including airflow limitation, gas trapping, and ventilation–perfusion mismatch.
  2. Identify appropriate evidence-based treatments for COPD when presented with scenario-based clinical questions.
  3. Analyze COPD patient scenarios to determine treatment priorities and justify the sequence of interventions.
