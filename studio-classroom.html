Description

This course reviews the fundamentals of COPD pathophysiology and the evidence-based treatment of the COPD patient — from recognizing airflow limitation and gas trapping to prioritizing interventions in real transport scenarios.

Objectives

  1. Define the core pathophysiologic mechanisms of COPD.
  2. Identify appropriate evidence-based treatments in scenario-based questions.
  3. Analyze COPD scenarios to prioritize interventions.
