IBSC Renewal — Catalog Versioning

How FOAMfrat can keep adding new courses to the IBSC renewal without ever disturbing the progress of students who are mid-cycle. (Admin view)

How it works

photo_camera

Versions snapshot the catalog

Each published version freezes the exact course list and hour requirements at that moment. Adding a course never edits a published version — it creates the next one.

lock

Students lock on enrollment

When a provider starts a renewal, they're pinned to the version that's current that day — for their full 4-year cycle. Their requirements can't shift under them.

verified

New courses ship safely

Publishing a new version only changes what new enrollees receive. Everyone already in progress stays exactly where they were.
